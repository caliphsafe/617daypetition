June 17th — #617Day — is more than a date. It’s a celebration of Boston’s heartbeat: our neighborhoods, our creativity, our businesses, and our history—anchored in the iconic 617 area code.


We’re asking Boston and Massachusetts to officially recognize June 17th as an annual civic celebration starting this year, with inclusive programming designed for all Bostonians and real benefits for local businesses and cultural institutions.


What #617Day can look like: $6.17 admission at participating museums/zoos/gardens, $6.17 specials (or 61.7% promotions) at local restaurants and shops, neighborhood storytelling, artist showcases, and a flagship gathering near Copley Square/Boylston that brings the city together.


Why it matters: It boosts small business revenue, strengthens civic pride, supports equitable economic opportunity, and spotlights Boston stories—From Our Streets to the World.
