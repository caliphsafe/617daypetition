We call upon the City of Boston and the Commonwealth of Massachusetts to formally declare the 17th day of June as #617Day, a yearly observance honoring our neighborhoods, our culture, and the small businesses that sustain the spirit of our city.


On this day, participating cultural institutions may offer admission at $6.17, while local restaurants and retail shops present special promotions in celebration, uniting the people and strengthening our local trade.


Add your name in support, and let it be known that the people stand for #617Day as an enduring Boston tradition.
